WATERMELON RECIPES
FRUIT PIZZA

Ingredients:

1/2 small round seedless watermelon .

1 kiwi fruit, peeled, halved, sliced.

1 small banana, peeled, sliced.

4 strawberries, hulled, chopped.

1/2 x 125g blueberries.

1/4 cup fresh mint leaves.

1 tablespoon toasted coconut chips.

2 tablespoons honey.

Method of preparation:

• Step 1 Cut a 2cm thick round from the cut end of the watermelon. Reserve remaining watermelon for another use. Cut watermelon round into 8 equal wedges.

• Step 2 Arrange wedges on a serving platter to resemble a pizza. Top with kiwi, banana, yoghurt, strawberries, blueberries, mint and coconut chips. Drizzle with honey. Serve immediately.

MELONADE

Ingredients:

70g (1/3 cup) caster sugar.

250ml (1 cup) boiling water.

1.6kg piece seedless watermelon, rind removed, chopped.

150ml lemon juice.

125ml (1/2 cup) white rum.

80ml (1/3 cup) citrus vodka.

1 lemon, thinly sliced.

Ice-cubes, to serve.

Method of preparation:

• Step 1 Place the sugar and water in a heatproof bowl. Stir until the sugar dissolves. Set aside to cool completely.

• Step 2 Meanwhile, process the watermelon in a food processor until smooth. Strain through a sieve into a bowl. Discard the solids. Stir in the lemon juice, rum, vodka, lemon slices and sugar syrup.

• Step 3 Fill serving glasses with ice-cubes and mint sprigs. Pour over the watermelon mixture. Stir to combine.
